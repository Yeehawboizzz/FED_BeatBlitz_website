THE ECHO CHAMBER'S ORIGIN

In 2019, The Echo Chamber embarked on its journey as an independent record
label with the establishment of TEC Records. While not limited by genre,
TEC Records aims to spotlight emerging and progressive sounds by Asian artists
rooted in the musical styles of jazz, hip hop, soul, or electronic.
The label also strives to use TEC Records as an archival imprint to
document the sounds of this part of the region, with a primary focus
on presenting these musical creations through vinyl records.
